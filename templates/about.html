About Me

Education and background

I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from Western Governors University in 2023. Before I went back to school, I was studying abroad to be an audio engineer in Vancouver, BC. It was during that time that I discovered an interest in technology.

What I do

I'm a programmer and educator. My favorite programming language is Python and I've used it to build websites, train AI, and perform data analysis on a wide variety of interests. I currently teach Python to kids in after-school clubs where we've built traffic systems, web servers, and fun drawing projects - all with Python.

My mission

Learning to code is just like learning anything else. It takes patience, time, and determination. No one learns this stuff in a day or in a semester. In my experience, I've learned that the reason many potential programmers forgo STEM is because, at some point, they were made to feel embarassed about their intelligence in math, science, or their first attempt at coding. Learning to code should be accessible , and cirriculum should be comprehensive.
